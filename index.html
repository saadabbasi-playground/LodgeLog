Working from home 70c/hr

You need a contemporaneous record of actual hours (timesheet, roster or diary kept through the year) plus at least one bill for each expense type the rate covers. Estimates aren't accepted.
WFH deduction$0.00

The rate covers electricity/gas, internet, phone and stationery — don't claim those separately. Depreciating assets (desk, monitor, laptop) are claimed on top: items ≤ $300 in full, above that over their effective life. Add them as an expense under "Tools & equipment".

Actual method: apportion each real bill (energy, internet, phone) by your work-use percentage, with records for the full year. Usually only worth it for heavy, well-documented home use.

Car expenses 88c/km

Normal home-to-work commuting doesn't count. For rideshare, kilometres while logged in and driving for work generally do.
Capped at 5,000 km per car. The rate covers ALL running costs — fuel, rego, insurance, servicing, depreciation. Keep a diary or app record of how you worked out the kilometres.
